What Users Get (via LIA through WhatsApp)

LIA ensures a comprehensive and supportive user experience, delivering personalized health guidance directly through WhatsApp. For individuals managing chronic conditions like diabetes, consistent daily habits and self-care are crucial. Our program provides the essential reminders and personalized support needed to empower employees to take control of their health.

1

Onboarding & Personalized Setup

Users join a WhatsApp group for community support and receive a dedicated 1-1 chat with their assigned coach. They choose DMP or DPP, and their coach helps tailor the program to their specific needs.

2

Daily Check-ins & Reminders

LIA sends consistent messages to remind users to log weight or glucose, take medications, and stay on track with healthy habits. These daily nudges are vital for building and maintaining self-care routines, especially for chronic disease management.

3

Chat-Based Tracking & 1-1 Feedback

Users can easily reply with their health data (e.g., glucose readings, weight). Their dedicated coach provides immediate, personalized feedback and guidance in their private 1-1 chat, helping them understand their progress and adjust behaviors.

4

Monthly Coaching Call

Scheduled video calls with their coach offer in-depth discussions to review progress, address challenges, and reset goals. This personal connection reinforces accountability and provides expert advice.

5

Personalized Content Sharing

Relevant recipes, health tips, and short educational lessons are shared via chat as accessible PDFs, images, or voice notes. Content is curated to support individual self-care and disease management.

6

Monthly Progress Summaries

Coaches provide encouraging summaries of progress and outline next steps, helping users visualize their improvements and stay motivated on their self-care journey.
